About Venue

About Venue's Banquet Hall- Banyan Tree Retreat is one of the focused activities managed by MAK Projects, which is ideal for Business meetings and conferences. It is a top-clat Venues gated and guarded community consisting 300 premium villas surrounding by a large Space for Business Meetings in Hyderabad.

It is idealistic in every aspect comprising modern facilities, world-class amenities and distinctive infrastructure. The Banquet Hall-Banyan Tree Retreat offers highly flexible Event venues in Hyderabad, which is a perfect choice for both corporate and social events. Some of its meeting and events facility includes birthday parties, wedding functions as well as seminars Business meetings and conferences.

Capacity - 150 to 300 pax

Type of Venues - Banquet Hall

Types of Events - Parties, Birthday Parties, Anniversaries, Conferences

Amenities - A/V Equipment, Catering Available, Pool, Rooms Available, Smoking Allowed, Valet Parking, WiFi

The Banquet Hall-Banyan Tree Retreat has its room dimensions of 3450 square feet, a tremendous destination for Business meetings and conferences, which is constantly based on Strategy and Knowledge.

It also specify numerous recreational facilities such as Nine Hole Executive Golf Course, Resort Nestles, Landscaped Gardens, and Avenue Plantation equipped with the State-of-the-art Street Lighting and Solar Fencing, providing jogging track, Poly clinic as well as Broadband connectivity for Business meetings and conferences.

It comprises worldwide famed architectural firm of SAA International Sdn Bhd backed by extraordinary interior and contemporary lifestyle. The architects has network of associate offices across the world including Singapore, Brunei, Indonesia, Malaysia, Vietnam and China.

Having such a splendid view it also offers three exquisite villas named as Cedar, Maple and Oak Villas with a Club House, Golf Course, Recreational Facility, Commercial Space and Apartments promising to provide a luxurious lifestyle with the classics of relaxation and entertainment.

Banyan Tree Retreat is conveniently located on the Srisalam highway at Maheswaram offers a higher level of luxury and user-friendly living environment. It takes 15 minutes to reach Hyderabad International Airport and 5 minutes to the Outer Ring Road from the Hotel which makes it an easy accessible venue to all the major routes of the city.
